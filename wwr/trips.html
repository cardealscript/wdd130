Our Rafting Trips

Half Day Adventure

Perfect for beginners and families. This 4-hour trip introduces you to exciting but manageable rapids while enjoying stunning natural scenery. Our professional guides ensure safety and fun throughout the journey.

Full Day Expedition

Spend a full day navigating diverse rapids, relaxing river sections, and breathtaking landscapes. Includes lunch break by the river and advanced safety briefing for intermediate adventurers.

Extreme Rapids Challenge

Designed for experienced thrill seekers. This intense 6-hour adventure features powerful Class IV rapids, teamwork challenges, and maximum adrenaline for those ready to push their limits.
