Horizonte Selvagem Rafting

We create adventures that become unforgettable memories. Each rapid is a chance to test limits, build friendships, and honor nature. Our mission is to deliver safe, thrilling experiences led by passionate river guides. Motto: "Adventure with safety, thrills with responsibility."

History

Founded by nature lovers and thrill seekers, Horizonte Selvagem Rafting was born from the desire to share the excitement of whitewater rafting with the world. From small local trips to larger expeditions, we have grown with one goal: to offer safety, fun, and lasting memories for every adventurer who joins us.
