Horizonte Selvagem Rafting

We create adventures that become unforgettable memories. Each rapid is a chance to test limits, build friendships, and honor nature. Our mission is to deliver safe, thrilling experiences led by passionate river guides. Motto: "Adventure with safety, thrills with responsibility."

History

Founded by nature lovers and thrill seekers, Horizonte Selvagem Rafting was born from the desire to share the excitement of whitewater rafting with the world. From small local trips to larger expeditions, we have grown with one goal: to offer safety, fun, and lasting memories for every adventurer who joins us. Over time, our passion for rivers and adventure transformed simple outings into unforgettable journeys. Each expedition reflects our commitment to responsibility, teamwork, and respect for nature. Today, Horizonte Selvagem Rafting welcomes families, friends, and solo travelers, offering experiences that combine adrenaline, safety, and the joy of discovering the wild spirit of water.
